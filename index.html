Adopeted May 31 2022

This is a fun tribute page to our loyal companions!

Chester is a cheetoh-cat-breed The Cheetoh cat incorporates the distinctive spotted coat characteristics from both the Ocicat and the Bengal breeds. notably that of harnessing the pronounced spotting from the Bengal breed and the taller longer legged stature from the Ocicat breed.

  1. Super affectionate nature and real love for their human companions
  2. Great deal of intelligence with the ability to learn quickly
  3. A keen instinct to hunt
